About Me

I completed my Diploma in Design Foundation Studies and I am currently in my third and final year of my Bachelor of Fine Arts (Honours) in Digital Arts.

My work experience is varied. I started my carrier as a Junior Designer with a design firm and then went on to manage my own Graphic Design and Marketing department at a multi-brand fashion company. I also work as a freelancer and have worked on branding, web design, social media marketing and publication design.
